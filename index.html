Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about buying, selling, and investing in real estate

How do I know if I'm ready to buy a home?
You're ready to buy a home when you have stable income, a good credit score (typically 620+), a down payment saved (usually 10-20%), and plan to stay in the area for at least 3–5 years. Our team can also help assess your financial readiness.
What's the best time of year to sell my home?
Spring and early summer are usually the busiest seasons, but the best time depends on your local market and goals.
How much should I offer on a house?
It depends on market conditions, comparable sales, and the property's condition. We'll help you determine a competitive offer.
What are closing costs and who pays them?
Closing costs include taxes, lender fees, insurance, and legal fees. Both buyer and seller may have different responsibilities.
Should I get a home inspection?
Yes. A professional inspection helps uncover hidden issues before completing the purchase.
How long does it take to buy or sell a home?
Most transactions take between 30 and 60 days depending on financing and negotiations.
What makes EliteHomes different from other real estate companies?
We combine local expertise, personalized service, and innovative technology to deliver outstanding results.
